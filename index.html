WELCOME TO GSPT & ASSOCIATES LLP

Global Service Provider & Team

GSPT & Associates LLP is a Chartered Accountancy (CA) firm established in January 2016 with its head office located in Delhi, India. The firm has 5 partners who bring in their specialized skills and expertise to provide a wide range of professional services to its clients.


The firm has 4 branches located across India, which enables it to serve clients from different parts of the country. The partners of the firm have specialized skills like LLB (Bachelor of Laws), DISA (Diploma in Information System Audit), FAFD (Forensic Accounting and Fraud Detection), CCCAB (Certificate Course on Concurrent Audit of Banks), and Financial Modeling.
