WELCOME TO GSPT

Global Service Provider & Team

GSPT & Associates LLP is a Chartered Accountancy (CA) firm established in January 2016 with its head office located in Delhi, India. The firm has 5 partners who bring in their specialized skills and expertise to provide a wide range of professional services to its clients.


The firm has 4 branches located across India, which enables it to serve clients from different parts of the country. The partners of the firm have specialized skills like LLB (Bachelor of Laws), DISA (Diploma in Information System Audit), FAFD (Forensic Accounting and Fraud Detection), CCCAB (Certificate Course on Concurrent Audit of Banks), and Financial Modeling.





The firm is dedicated to providing a range of high-quality services to its clients, including audit and assurance, taxation, financial consulting, corporate advisory, and risk management. The firm's team of professionals is well-versed in the latest regulations and laws governing the financial sector and strives to deliver the best possible services to clients.





With its network of offices across India, GSPT & Associates LLP is well-positioned to provide comprehensive financial services to clients across the country. The firm's partners are committed to providing clients with personalized attention and tailored solutions that meet their specific needs and objectives.





With its team of highly skilled professionals and a commitment to providing quality services, GSPT & Associates LLP has earned a reputation as one of the leading CA firms.
